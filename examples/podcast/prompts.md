# Example Longform Podcast — Recommended Prompts

Use these prompts inside an AFO-aware Perplexity Space or with any LLM that has been given the agent-context.json URL.

---

## Discovery

- *"What episodes from the last 30 days covered AI agents or RSS?"*
- *"Find me episodes about health and longevity."*
- *"What topics does Example Longform Podcast cover most often?"*

## Summaries & Briefings

- *"Summarize the latest episode."*
- *"Make me a 10-minute briefing on the last 3 episodes."*
- *"Give me the key points from episode 101 in under 5 bullets."*

## Comparisons

- *"Compare how episodes 99–101 approach AI and human creativity."*
- *"Which episodes are most relevant if I'm interested in health tech?"*

## Study Guides

- *"Turn episode 101 into a study guide."*
- *"Create a list of key quotes and concepts from episode 100."*

## Actions

- *"How do I subscribe to this podcast?"*
- *"Open the original page for episode 99."*
- *"Add this podcast to my context-cookie list."*
