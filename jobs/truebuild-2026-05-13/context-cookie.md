## What Is a Context Cookie?

A context cookie is a small file that sits on your website and gives AI assistants a persistent memory of who you are. Instead of reconstructing your business description from scratch every time someone asks about you, the AI reads this file and gets your identity directly — in your words, with your facts, your history, and your positioning.

Think of it as a business card that every AI assistant can read automatically.
