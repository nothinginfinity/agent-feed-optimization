## What's Missing — And What Each Gap Is Costing You

---

### ❌ RSS / News Feed

**What it does:** Lets AI assistants subscribe to your latest content and surface it in answers when people ask about business credit. When you publish a new article, success story, or program update, AI tools that check feeds will surface it automatically.

**What you're missing:** Every day, small business owners ask AI assistants "what's new in business credit building?" or "are there any good programs for building corporate credit?" TrueBuild has been operating since 2001 with thousands of success stories — but none of that reach AI assistants as fresh, surfaceable content. You're invisible in the one place where your ideal customer is actively asking questions.

**Score lift if added:** +3 points

---

### ❌ Sitemap

**What it does:** Tells AI assistants (and search engines) exactly which pages exist on your site, so nothing important gets missed.

**What you're missing:** AI assistants are guessing what's on your site. If you have program details, FAQs, pricing pages, or success stories on other pages, they may never be discovered or cited — because there's no map.

**Score lift if added:** +2 points

---

### ❌ llms.txt

**What it does:** A plain-text file written specifically for AI systems, describing who you are, what you do, how long you've been doing it, and how you want to be described when an AI recommends you.

**What you're missing:** When someone asks ChatGPT "what is TrueBuild Business Credit?", the AI has to guess from scattered web data. It might describe you accurately, or it might not — you have no control. A llms.txt file is your direct line to every AI assistant, in your own words. For a company with 20+ years of history and 48,000 clients, this is the fastest credibility signal you're not using.

**Score lift if added:** +3 points

---

### ❌ Agent Context File (agent-context.json)

**What it does:** A structured data file with your exact business details — company name, description, services, phone number, hours, how to get started, and what types of businesses you serve.

**What you're missing:** When a small business owner asks an AI assistant "how do I contact TrueBuild?" or "what does TrueBuild's program include?", the AI has to scrape your homepage and guess. With an agent-context.json file, the AI reads your answers directly — accurate, complete, and in your voice. The phone number `(800) 730-9490`, the 90–120 day timeline, the 7-step process — all of that becomes instantly citable.

**Score lift if added:** +3 points

---

### ❌ Agent Policy File (agent-policy.json)

**What it does:** Tells AI systems your rules — what they're allowed to quote, how to attribute TrueBuild, and what they should not claim on your behalf.

**What you're missing:** AI assistants may describe your program in ways you haven't approved — overpromising results, misquoting timelines, or attributing competitor claims to you. A policy file gives you machine-readable protection. For a financial services business where compliance and accurate representation matter, this is more than a nice-to-have.

**Score lift if added:** +2 points

---

### ❌ Agent Actions File (agent-actions.json)

**What it does:** Lets AI assistants take the next step on behalf of a customer — start a free business credit analysis, fill out a contact form, or call your team directly.

**What you're missing:** Right now, an AI assistant can describe TrueBuild and then the conversation ends. The customer has to leave the AI, find your site, and figure out what to do next — and most won't. An agent-actions.json file lets the AI say "I can start your free business credit analysis right now" and link or initiate directly. For a lead-generation business, this is the highest-value file on this list.

**Score lift if added:** +2 points

---

### ❌ Context Memory File (context-cookie.json)

**What it does:** A persistent identity packet that lets AI assistants remember who TrueBuild is across sessions — consistently describing and recommending you the same way, every time, to every user.

**What you're missing:** Every AI conversation about business credit starts from scratch. There's no persistent signal reinforcing TrueBuild's 20+ year history, 48,000-client track record, or "Internet's longest-standing" positioning. A competitor who adds this file first owns that consistent AI recommendation slot.

**Score lift if added:** +3 points
